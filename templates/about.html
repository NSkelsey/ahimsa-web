AboutSome info for you!


WARNING! This is experimental software. Do not rely on it for anything that may endanger you or anyone around you.

This is the public facing website for a node in the Ombuds peer-to-peer network. More information is available at the project's website. We have answered some questions below.

Definitions

Block chain The public ledger of every bitcoin transaction.

Bulletin A message stored in the Bitcoin blockchain.

Topic A metadata tag used to group similar bulletins together.

Author A bitcoin address from which bulletins originate.

Endorsement A statement indicating approval or support of a bulletin.


FAQsBecause we know this is confusing.

What are you trying to do again?

Demonstrate another usecase for Bitcoin and generate a better archive of public speech.

Why?

Bitcoin has been trumpted as a tool for finacial freedom. We want to use it to empower the fourth estate.

Isn't it expensive and rude to store data in the blockchain?

Yes and yes. All told it will be about 12 cents to store a single tweet in the blockchain. But there are a couple methods to reduce that cost further.

We are aware of how broken it all is and we are working on it. Have an ice pop.
